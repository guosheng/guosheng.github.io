My research interests are in machine learning and computer vision applications, particularly in visual recognition problems.
Before moving to NTU Singapore, I was a research fellow at Australian Centre for Robotic Vision (ACRV) and Australian Centre for Visual Technologies (ACVT),  
closely working with Prof. Chunhua shen, Prof. Ian Reid, and Prof. Anton van den Hengel.
I completed my PhD degree at the University of Adelaide (2010-2014), supervised by Prof. Chunhua Shen (principal),  Prof. David Suter and Dr.Tat-Jun Chin. 
I received my bachelor and master degree from South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2007 and 2010, respectively.
